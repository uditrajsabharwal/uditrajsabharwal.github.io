Love…what is love?

It’s that smile that she wears on her face

Love…what is love?

It’s her long hair

Love…what is love?

It’s her dark eyes

Love…what is love?

It’s her soft lips

Love…what is love?

It’s your hearts song

Love…what is love?

It’s nothing and yet it’s everything
